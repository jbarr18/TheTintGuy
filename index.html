Benefits of Automotive Window Tint

Heat Rejection

"After tinting your windows, the first thing you’ll notice is how much darker they are (although that won’t compromise your visibility on the road). Nobody will be able to see what’s inside the vehicle, which will likely deter someone who would’ve broken into your car. Along with added privacy, ceramic window tint will also help you and your passengers avoid an injury from shards of glass. While it won’t prevent your windows from breaking in the event of an accident, ceramic window tint will keep glass from shattering."

UV Protection

Our films also protect you and your vehicle against 99% of harmful UV rays from the sun. These UV rays are known to cause damage to our eyes, skin, and the interior of your vehicle. Most films offer this protection nowadays. I instead suggest you inquire about the Infrared Heat Rejection of a film. Higher quality films like ceramic or carbon ceramic blends will offer the highest IR rejection.

Privacy and Safety

Asthetic

Give your vehicle a stylish and sleek appearance that enhances the overall aesthetic appeal. Tinted windows add depth and dimensionality to your cars exterior, making it look more sophisticated and modern.

Quality

Ceramic window tint may cost a bit more than other films, but a quality installation from professionals will make that investment worth it. And because ceramic window tint is made up of tiny nanoparticles that are bound tightly together, it’s highly durable. When you choose quality tinted car windows, you’ll experience better privacy without signal interference. Plus, you’ll keep your interior cool, protect your skin and eyes from the sun, and stop running your A/C so much!
